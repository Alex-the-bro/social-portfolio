About Me

My name is Alex Mwansa. An experienced social media community manager and content creator. I have a passion for creating digital content and analysing the enagement data of my own creations for insights on how content can be improved or redesigned.

Throughout my 2 years+ working in the field, I have administered and contributed to serveral social media competitions and campaigns. Through this, I've developed the skills required to create high quality content that returns engagement and brand awareness. I strategically plan, manage and execute the publishing of digital content across various social media platforms with the aim to appeal to relevant social groups.

I also manage public interactions between a brand and its followers on social media by engaging in conversations, attending to queries and providing realistic solutions based on the essential product or company knowledge I have. The goal in doing this is to build an authentic online community that consists of a companies customers,partners, associates and even employers in some cases.

Content Creation

Creating written, video & graphic social media content that drives engagement and awareness.

Content Scheduling

Strategic Planning

Developing a content plan that is designed to attract your target audience, ensuring that the target group is engaged before, during and after an interaction.

Community Management

Defining and monitoring the methods an organisation uses to engage with its audience on different platforms.

Front-End Development

Building single and multipage page websites using front end development languages such as HTML, CSS & JavaScript backed by frameworks and libraries such as JQuery, Bootstrap and React.JS
